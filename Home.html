About Us

Ending Pet Homelessness

  • TRP (The Right Pawtm ) is committed to being a helping paw to our good friend PAWS (The Philippine Animal Welfare Society). And bring awareness to domestic animals in need, especially our furry friends in shelters that are struggling to take care of them financially.

  • 80% of our profit is donated to PAWS and the promotion of humane treatment of all animals. Every stuffed animal you buy gives a rough estimate of about a week's worth of food for one rescued animal.

INFORMATION/FAQ

How can I order a stuffed animal?

  • From our home tab, click the plushies tab and pick a furr-baby to take home! (max of 5 due to prohibition of bulk orders and reselling). Once you have filled your heart and cart with these adorable pups and kits, direct yourself to the cart tab and process your order inputting all the needed information.

  • Arranging your order takes approximately (1-2 working days)

  • Creation/Manufacturing of your stuffed animal (2-5 working days) each. (depending to size as well)

  • Shipping/Delivery is not assured since we only use local delivery services. (We’ll move to Shopee if there is a popular demand!) But we guarantee we will ship your order as soon as possible.

Does my purchase go directly to charity?

  • We assure you that most of your donations will greatly help the PAWS administration. However we need some of the resources to fund our hard workers who make the handmade animals you know and love!

Are you an adoption shelter?

  • The Right Paws TM is a non-profit organization, we are not a branch of The Philippine Animal Welfare Society. Nonetheless we dedicate our work to being a helping hand in saving animals in need of a loving home.

What are the stuffed animals made of?

  • All stuffed animals are cruelty-free and made of organic and natural fibers, such as cotton and wool. These are all hand-made that’s why we ask for your patience and support as we are a volunteer based organization with limited time, staff, and resources.

How else can we donate/Do you accept donations?

  • As of now we do not accept donations, other than your much appreciated purchase of our stuffed animals! You may support us by sharing our business with others in hopes to raise more awareness for our fluffy friends!

Why is my order taking too long?

  • We deeply apologize for the inconveniences, as a startup business with only limited time, funds, and staff– We cannot prioritize too many orders at a time but we assure you that your plushies will be delivered to you safely once they have been completed and shipped.
